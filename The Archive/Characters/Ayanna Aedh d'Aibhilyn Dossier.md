## PREAMBLE - WHAT THIS FILE IS FOR

*This is not the Vitrified Wing infrastructure record. That file, if acquired in full, would say what it needs to say about Project Ennea, the coherence-substrate program, and the institutional chain that produced Subject E-1.*

*It is not the Lyréne Pharmaceuticals corporate record. That document would describe a junior research associate, a promising neural-interface diagnostics specialist, and an Auroran Redactor with a conditional Mark file. It would be correct in several surface details and false by omission in nearly every way that matters.*

*It is not a Precursor artifact brief. The Sibyl Fragment requires its own record. Ayanna is not the artifact, not its property, and not merely a technical solution to its interface problem.*

*This file exists because Ayanna Aedh d'Aibhilyn has become too specific, too active, and too personally consequential to be treated as a program output. She is an engineered subject, a registered Redactor, a daughter, a friend, a researcher, and an investigator. The operational failure now emerging around her is simple: the Vitrified Wing succeeded at making a person, and that person has begun examining the architecture of the life built around her.*

*The methodological problem is also obvious. The available record is contaminated by institutional concealment, deliberate social placement, false family history, and hostile suppression activity. The file should therefore be read as a character dossier first and a program analysis second.*

## II. WHAT SHE IS

Ayanna is the Vitrified Wing's first successful biological coherence interface: a human being engineered to provide the recursive density required for a Precursor diagnostic instrument of the Sibyl class to accept input.

She is not a clone. She is not a vessel. She is not a mature-grown subject. She was gestated, born, raised, educated, and socialized like an ordinary Auroran child because the program required a genuine person rather than a laboratory-trained instrument.

Her genome was specified across approximately fourteen hundred parameters, with emphasis on cognitive coherence, sensory acuity, structural pattern recognition, metabolic efficiency, psychological stability, and the capacity to sustain unusually clean recursive operations. The Wing did not intend to produce merely a useful body. It intended to produce a person whose mind could survive contact with something baseline humans cannot reliably interpret.

This is the central ethical fact of the case. The Wing's success makes its violation worse, not lesser. Ayanna is psychologically real. Her feelings are real. Her loyalties are real. Her citizenship is real. The life around her is not a stage set, but it has been handled at nearly every intimate point.

A second subject exists under the same program, still in early childhood.

Ayanna does not know this.

## III. APPEARANCE, TEMPERAMENT, AND SOCIAL PRESENCE

Ayanna is tall for an Auroran woman and slender in a way that reads as efficient rather than fragile. Her body has the quiet precision of something designed for metabolic economy and neuromuscular coordination, though she experiences this simply as being naturally good at movement. Since adolescence, she has been unusually capable at physical activity, and the ease of her own body has always been one of her private comforts.

Her hair is a light green, almost lime, catching Valerie's marble-reflected moonlight with a faint silvery cast. Her eyes are verdant-gold, an unusual pigmentation associated with the visual-sensory parameters specified for her cohort. The Vitrified Wing justified the trait as useful for perceiving Sibyl-class visual artifacts, but the choice also reveals the designers' pride: Ayanna was made functional, but she was also made beautiful.

Her skin is medium-fair, common to the central Auroran cantons. Her features are clean, slightly long, and almost too symmetrical. The Wing deliberately introduced small asymmetries during fetal development so she would not unsettle those around her: her left eye sits fractionally higher than her right, and one side of her lower lip is slightly fuller. Ayanna does not know either feature was specified.

She moves with a precision people often mistake for training. It is not training. It is the outward expression of a body whose proprioception and coordination were specified before birth.

Temperamentally, Ayanna is stable, curious, generally cheerful, and genuinely attached to her life in Valerie. She loves her work in neural-interface diagnostics, and that work is not false. She contributes to real medical research and is good at it. Her colleagues like her. Her public life is not a stage set. The city is real. The job is real. Her affection for Aurora is real.

She lives in a small, well-kept apartment overlooking a public garden. She owns two plants: one healthy, one uncertain. She runs in the mornings before the Moon's zenith, when Valerie is quiet and the marble catches the low silver light. These details matter because they are not engineered in the same way her origin was. They are the life she has made inside the structure built around her.

What is changing is her relationship to the people who shaped that life. Ayanna has begun to feel that she has been *handled*. She cannot yet describe the scale of the manipulation, but she knows the texture of it. That awareness is making her more cautious, more observant, and harder than the Wing expected.

## IV. NAME AND COVER IDENTITY

Ayanna's name was designed to read as old-canton Auroran.

The **d'** particle marks a constructed old-canton lineage. The three-name structure makes her socially legible as the daughter of a small, old-canton family recently rooted in Valerie. This explains why she has no visible extended family and why the family history appears sparse without immediately seeming suspicious.

The middle name **Aedh** is an old Auroran-language word meaning approximately *flame* or *bright thing held in the hand*. **Ayanna** is rare but documented. **d'Aibhilyn** was constructed for her; no pre-cohort family of that name exists in Auroran civic records.

Ayanna believes her parents chose the name because it was lyrical.

In truth, the Wing chose it and assigned it to the people who raised her.

## V. THE LIFE BUILT AROUND HER

The Vitrified Wing chose not to raise Ayanna in a fabricated cradle environment. Earlier program failures showed that a recursive singularity dense enough to interface with the Sibyl Fragment could not be produced by obvious artificiality. Ayanna needed a real city, real routines, real education, and relationships that would feel true under scrutiny.

The solution was not to build a fake world. The Wing placed her inside the real one and manipulated the people closest to her.

### V.1 The Aibhilyns

Two senior Lyréne scientists were selected to serve as Ayanna's adoptive parents. They assumed the Aibhilyn identity, moved into a real Valerie civic district, raised her in an ordinary home, sent her to real schools, and became her parents in the practical and emotional sense.

They were not told the full purpose of the project. They knew she came from a coherence-engineered substrate and that normal childhood development was necessary to preserve her viability as a research subject. They were instructed to raise her, guide her toward Lyréne, and transition her gradually into research participation under non-traumatic protocols.

They did the job well enough that it became more than a job. They raised a daughter. Ayanna loves them. They love her. The tragedy is that this love exists inside an operational structure that may eventually ask them to deliver her into circumstances they do not fully understand.

### V.2 Childhood and Friendships

Ayanna attended real schools and grew up among real Auroran children. However, three of her four closest childhood friendships were placed deliberately: children of Wing personnel positioned in her social path to shape her emotional development and keep her stable.

The fourth friendship was unplanned. **Elise**, a real Auroran child with no connection to Lyréne, attached to Ayanna in primary school and became her closest friend. The Wing monitored the friendship for two years, then allowed it to continue because Elise regulated Ayanna emotionally in ways the placed friendships could not.

Elise is now the only person Ayanna trusts without reservation.

### V.3 The Boyfriend

Ayanna's former boyfriend was a Vitrified Wing operative. The relationship lasted three years and ended eight months ago in what Ayanna believed was an amicable breakup. He was tender, attentive, intelligent, and almost perfectly suited to her needs.

Recently, this perfection has begun to bother her. The relationship no longer feels fully real in memory. Not because her grief was false, but because the man himself had too few edges. He was too calibrated. She has not concluded that he was placed in her life, but she has stopped grieving him.

### V.4 Education and Research Track

Ayanna was raised in a household where science was ordinary conversation. By adolescence, she was already exceptionally fluent in the vocabulary of research. When she tested unusually high on cognitive assessments, her parents guided her into Lyréne's youth research engagement program.

The program was real. Other children participated. Ayanna's contributions were genuine. Under that same cover, the Vitrified Wing conducted coherence monitoring, neurological diagnostics, and stimulus protocols designed to map her developing substrate.

By the time she entered the Valerie School of Natural Philosophy at sixteen and joined Lyréne's junior research staff at twenty, the path felt natural.

It had been built across her whole childhood.

## VI. REDACTORY PROFILE

### VI.1 Manifestation and Registration

Ayanna manifested as a Redactor at nineteen, during a structural-mechanics laboratory exercise. A torsion test rig stopped at a load where it should have failed, and she knew before checking that it had stopped because she had made it stop.

She registered through Aurora's framework and was assessed by the Apocachynthion's Auroran embassy. The assessment classified her as a **Mechanism Anchor**, confirmed Quill rank, and sealed **Mark 2** under conditional Auroran-territory Margin.

The classification is accurate but incomplete.

### VI.2 Anchor

Ayanna's Anchor aligns with the structural-relational behavior of physical systems: force, motion, stress, load, energy translation, and mechanical causality. She perceives how systems operate, not merely what they look like.

Her case was unusual from intake. Most Mechanism Redactors first express through action, then learn refined perception. Ayanna expressed perceptually from the beginning. She could see force vectors, inertial paths, stress distributions, energy gradients, and mechanical causal chains with exceptional clarity.

Her domain breadth was also too developed for a newly manifested Quill. She could affect simple kinematic systems, redistribute structural stress, manage energy translation, and engage multi-system mechanical configurations at a level closer to a trained Stylus than a new intake case. The Apocachynthion noted this but did not escalate the case.

### VI.3 Capability Profile

**Passive perception:** Ayanna can perceive the mechanical structure of her environment in real time: force distribution, inertial pathing, architectural stress, electrical and thermal flow, and causal movement through physical systems. Under stress or fatigue, this mechanical layer can assert itself without her choosing it.

**Kinematic redirection:** She can alter the path of moving objects within a manageable mass and velocity range.

**Stress reconfiguration:** She can redistribute load across structures, strengthen elements by shifting stress elsewhere, or create localized failure points.

**Energy translation management:** She can briefly suspend, redirect, or accelerate energy already moving through a system, including electrical current, heat flow, or kinetic transfer.

**Mechanism suspension:** At depth, she can briefly halt a defined mechanical process. This is highly expensive and has produced Overreach symptoms during assessment.

**Reach:** In private experimentation, she has begun pushing toward biological-mechanical perception and resonance-adjacent perception. Biological perception works with risk. Resonance perception has produced partial successes she does not understand and has not reported.

### VI.4 Codex Truth

The Vitrified Wing did not deliberately create her Anchor. It engineered her substrate so narrowly toward structural pattern recognition and coherence that, when she manifested, **Mechanism** was the only stable attractor her recursion could consolidate around.

Her Mechanism perception is also the developmental prelude to her true intended function. She is practicing on Page-local mechanical systems the kind of structural reading the Sibyl Fragment will later demand at deeper levels of reality.

She does not know this.

The Wing does not fully know this.

The Apocachynthion does not know this.

## VII. THE SIBYL FRAGMENT

The Sibyl Fragment is the artifact Ayanna was made to interface with.

It is Precursor diagnostic infrastructure, not a divine object and not an Obscurial. Modern instruments cannot meaningfully read it. Baseline humans sometimes receive distorted, intermittent visions from it. A sufficiently dense recursive singularity can function as an actual receiver.

Ayanna was engineered to be such a receiver.

The Fragment was built for probabilistic site assessment: it returns structured information about coherence anomalies, thinness fluctuations, resonance events, and related structural conditions. Its output was meant for a Precursor-class observer. Ayanna's engineered substrate and Mechanism Anchor make her an accidental modern approximation of that role.

At sustained coupling, she may eventually perceive more than the Wing expects: not just local anomaly data, but traces of the wider Precursor stabilization network, the Lunar Crown's containment work, Vael'Theryn's modeling activity, and Myehnai's expelled Instrument of Legibility in the White Desert.

She will not understand all of what she sees.

She will see it anyway.

## VIII. IMPORTANT RELATIONSHIPS

### VIII.1 Mireille du Vauclain

Mireille is the Ennea Project Director and the woman who designed Ayanna's substrate. To Ayanna, she is her mother's closest friend at Lyréne: a senior researcher who has been present at family dinners for as long as Ayanna can remember.

Ayanna trusts Mireille in a way she does not entirely understand. Mireille has watched her with a gravity Ayanna is only now beginning to notice. Mireille is lying to her, but not in the same way the Aibhilyns are. Her lie carries guilt, attachment, and hesitation.

Mireille has refused, on multiple occasions, to authorize work that would harm Ayanna. The Vitrified Wing has noticed. Its patience with her is finite.

### VIII.2 The Aibhilyns

Ayanna's parents are not her biological parents, but they are the people who raised her. Their relationship with her is both operational and real. They helped build the lie around her. They also love her.

Their future importance lies in the moment when the project requires them to choose between obedience and the daughter they helped create as a person.

### VIII.3 Elise

Elise is Ayanna's closest friend and the only major childhood relationship not arranged by the Wing. She is emotionally important because she represents the portion of Ayanna's life that was allowed to become real without being designed first.

The Wing is now surveilling Elise more closely.

### VIII.4 Cael Rouvelle

Cael is the Wing's operations and security lead. He is the man who would terminate Ayanna if ordered. He is not sadistic and does not enjoy the work. He is competent, loyal, and increasingly aware that the containment model is failing.

He has authorized killings and suppressions around Ayanna's investigation. He has also urged the Council to accelerate the project's activation phase before the situation becomes unrecoverable.

### VIII.5 Lacroix de Velaine

Lacroix is the senior researcher attached to the Sibyl Fragment. He has studied it longer than any other living person and understands less than he once believed he did. He is likely to become important when Ayanna is brought into direct contact with the Fragment.

### VIII.6 Em'manuel Wonderwall / Project Seam

Project Seam identified Ayanna as anomalous through the institutional pattern around her: intake irregularity, Lyréne proximity, developmental profile, and non-standard trajectory. A brief reached Em'manuel Wonderwall eleven months ago.

Project Seam has not intervened. It is passively observing. The Vitrified Wing does not know this. Clockwork has no valid seam while competent Auroran and civil jurisdictions remain available.

## IX. WHAT SHE SUSPECTS

Ayanna has begun investigating inconsistencies in her life.

She currently suspects:

- Her medical history is unusual.
- Some of her friendships and opportunities were curated.
- Her former boyfriend's arrival and disappearance were not ordinary.
- Her parents are hiding something about her childhood.
- Mireille knows something important and will not say it.
- Her Redactor ability is too clean to be ordinary luck.
- Yvanne du Roux's death was not a domestic accident.

She does **not** yet suspect:

- That she was engineered before birth.
- That the Aibhilyn identity was constructed around her.
- That her parents were assigned to raise her.
- That an artifact exists for which she was designed.
- That the Vitrified Wing has manipulated nearly every intimate relationship in her life.
- That people are being killed to prevent her from finding the truth.

The gap between suspicion and truth is still large.

It is closing.

## X. THE INVESTIGATION

Over the last fourteen months, Ayanna has quietly begun checking the architecture of her own life.

She has noticed inconsistencies in childhood medical records, demographic clustering among her childhood friends, unexplained neurological diagnostics in her annual examinations, and gaps in her parents' supposed western-coastal-canton background. She has asked casual questions of colleagues and learned that some procedures described as standard for her are not standard for anyone else.

Her most dangerous step was contacting **Yvanne du Roux**, a young archivist at the Valerie Civic Records Bureau. Yvanne found a partial discrepancy in the Aibhilyn family record and flagged it for follow-up.

Eleven days later, Yvanne died in a staged domestic accident.

Ayanna attended the funeral. Her Mechanism perception told her the fall was plausible but improbable. She has no hard evidence, but the mechanics do not feel right to her.

Since then, she has become more careful. She no longer keeps written notes. She avoids sensitive queries on personal devices. She does her thinking in places where she can verify she is alone. The Wing has noticed the shift.

More recently, she contacted **Solène le Marais**, an investigative journalist with a history of institutional skepticism. The contact was indirect and cautious. The Wing intercepted the second exchange. Cael is now considering whether Solène must be removed.

## XI. WHAT THE WING HAS DONE

In the last fourteen months, the Vitrified Wing has authorized:

- Yvanne du Roux's murder, staged as a domestic accident.
- The relocation and identity replacement of Ayanna's former boyfriend.
- The quiet removal of two junior researchers who asked too many questions about Ayanna's diagnostic protocols.
- Increased surveillance of Ayanna's social contacts, including Elise.
- A standing plan to remove Solène le Marais if her contact with Ayanna escalates.
- A standing plan to terminate Ayanna herself, requiring Council authorization.

The situation has shifted from passive monitoring to active suppression. The Wing is still trying to preserve Ayanna as an asset, but it is also preparing for the possibility that she cannot be contained.

## XII. CURRENT CONFLICT TRAJECTORY

The core conflict is simple: Ayanna is investigating, the Wing is suppressing, and both sides are accelerating.

Likely narrative trajectories:

1. **Managed Revelation:** The Wing brings Ayanna into the Sibyl Fragment program early, revealing only enough truth to convert her into a cooperating asset.
2. **External Exposure:** Solène or another outside vector uncovers enough to trigger federal, Apocachynthion, Church, or corporate attention.
3. **Anchor Recognition:** Ayanna's Mechanism perception deepens until she can perceive the manipulative architecture around her without needing documents.
4. **External Seam Intervention:** Em'manuel decides the pattern around Ayanna requires lawful disclosure or protection, collapsing the Wing's model from outside.
5. **Apocachynthion Escalation:** Ayanna's deepening Anchor expression triggers institutional review, exposing irregularities the Wing cannot suppress.

The Wing is betting it can execute the first trajectory before any of the others complete.

That assumption is fragile.

## XIII. CURRENT ASSESSMENT

Ayanna's central character tension is not simply that she was engineered. It is that she was engineered into personhood so successfully that the people who made her now have to confront the fact that she is a person.

She was designed as an interface, but she became a daughter, friend, researcher, citizen, Redactor, and investigator. Her life is not false just because parts of it were arranged. That is what makes the violation worse. The Wing did not build a fake life and put her inside it. It inserted false hands into a real life and let her love what those hands touched.

Her story begins at the point where her own gift turns against the architecture that shaped her. Mechanism lets her see how systems work.

Her life is a system.

She is beginning to see it.

## XIV. OPEN QUESTIONS

1. **What happens when Ayanna confronts Mireille?** Mireille may lie, break, confess partially, or become an unauthorized informant. Every option damages the Wing.

2. **Can Ayanna perceive the Sibyl Fragment before physical contact?** The Fragment is held roughly ninety kilometers south of Valerie. It is not detectable by standard instruments, but Ayanna's perceptual development may not obey the Wing's models.

3. **What does the Fragment do in proximity to her?** It may align toward her, respond across distance, or begin producing output before the activation phase begins.

4. **Does Project Seam escalate first?** The Wing does not know it is being watched. If Seam alerts a competent Auroran, Apocachynthion, or civil authority, the entire containment architecture may collapse without warning.

5. **What becomes of the second subject?** The child under the same program is the Wing's institutional fallback. Ayanna does not know she exists.

6. **Can Ayanna remain herself after learning the truth?** The central danger is not merely external exposure. It is whether Ayanna can integrate the knowledge that she was made for a purpose without allowing that purpose to define the whole of who she is.
