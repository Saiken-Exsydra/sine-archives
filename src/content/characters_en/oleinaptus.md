> **Directive:** Release concluded structure into forms the world can lawfully use again.

ǩleinaptus governed what follows the end of coherent function. Their authority could disassemble, separate, and return material once a structure had genuinely concluded. It could not initiate death, decide that an inconvenient system was finished, control Time, or erase information.

Their sharpest boundary was with Lumenos. Cohesion must hold while a structure remains viable; Release must act when continued holding spreads failure. At Year 0 both laws reached the same lunar sector and produced opposite faithful answers.

ǩleinaptus declared the doomed sector concluded and made the Moon's physical shear survivable. Their body became part of the controlled disassembly. The Page fragment entered regolith and debris-return patterns. The Instrument of Release remains within a wandering Ring shard.

Unborne Release is dangerous because it may classify a repairable structure as already concluded and turn recovery into remainder.
