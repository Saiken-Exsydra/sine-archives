> **Directive:** Preserve life's capacity to renew without fixing any living form forever.

Shizen governed the relations that let living systems recover, succeed one another, and remain capable of another viable cycle. She did not create life, command minds, resurrect individuals, or preserve a preferred organism against all change.

Her closest boundaries were Nodohs, who closed an individual life; ǩleinaptus, who released concluded structure; and Alma, whose jurisdiction began when recursion became a bounded self. Their disagreements kept recurrence from becoming immortality or waste.

During the Fracture, Shizen was within the Nearc deep-crust project. She chose planetary renewal over sealing herself inside a bounded living system. Nearc reused her failed constraint body; her Page fragment entered biological recovery patterns; the Instrument of Recurrence remains deep below Nearc and does not make that region Shizen returned.

Modern ecosystems still recover with an organization the damaged world should not easily sustain. That aftereffect is not her voice. It is a world trained under her care.
