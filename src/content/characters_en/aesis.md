> **Directive:** Keep the exposed surface between the extremes that would make life impossible.

Aesis governed the habitable interval maintained by atmosphere, pressure, circulation, and radiative buffering. Weather was a consequence of her work, not a portfolio she could command without limit. She could not create air, water, light, heat, or life by herself.

She depended on Lumenos to keep atmosphere joined to Terra, Myehnai to make radiation legible to living receivers, and Shizen to define what biological recurrence required. Those dependencies made habitability a relation rather than a single law.

During the Fracture, Aesis occupied Ksy'rion's atmospheric interface. She vented the failing sky architecture through layered circulation instead of permitting one planetary shock. Her body diffused through that interface; her fragment entered climate regulation; the Instrument of Interval now moves through damaged Bright-Dark exchange architecture.

The modern world's unstable climate is not her anger. It is a habitable interval maintained by machinery and damaged aftereffects without her living judgment.
