## Lineage and the Ardeatan Exile

The name Engelmeyer is a fixture of the Empire's western band interior. However, Leonard's grandfather severed ties with the main house during the territorial disputes of the late 2110s, migrating across the border into the neighboring nation-state of Ardeatus. Adopting the Ardeatan "von-" particle to assimilate into their distinct social grammar, the expatriate branch established itself in the northern frontier city of Eraveth.

Leonard's parents were highly visible figures in Eraveth. His mother, **Elara ker-Vane**, was a brilliant material scientist at the Forge Academy of Kovansk, researching thermal thresholds in BrightCrystal stress fractures. His father, **Kaelen von-Engelmeyer**, was a fiercely vocal Ardeatan politician who built his career fighting the encroachment of imperial corporatism — specifically, Kaelen successfully blocked SiNE's Evanote division from extending the Imperial Arterial Rail into Eraveth's sovereign logistics network, arguing that corporate infrastructure was a prelude to corporate subjugation.

In 2160, when Leonard was fifteen, his parents were killed in what the Ardeatan authorities officially classified as a catastrophic domestic gas leak. The Apocachynthion Intelligence Branch has long held, but never formally pursued, the hypothesis that the explosion was a targeted assassination — likely orchestrated by corporate actors, possibly SiNE's unregistered CLOCKWORK division, to simultaneously remove a political obstacle and silence Elara's Forge research.

Leonard was arriving home the moment the explosion occurred. He should have died. He did not.

## The Incident and the Time Authorization

The explosion coincided precisely with a massive Stase-incursion rolling off the Darkmargin. The Stase — a cosmological anomaly where Time halts its traversal of reality — washed over the Engelmeyer home exactly as the gas ignited.

Leonard was caught inside the detonation. Because the Stase is a structured absence of Time, the explosion was frozen. Leonard found himself trapped in a subjective eternity of agony, feeling the initial microscopic threshold of burning gas but unable to die, unable to move, and unable to complete the sequence of the event.

He endured this traversal-suspended hell for an unknown subjective duration. His survival was catalyzed by the intrusion of an SOE — a traversal-independent entity that had inhabited a broken Ardeatan mechanical crow. The entity, which Leonard would later name **Kerz**, possessed a hyper-radiant coherence core. Kerz wandered into the frozen explosion.

The proximity of Kerz's localized coherence core catalyzed Leonard's latent Redactory. Desperate to escape the sensory overload, Leonard instinctively forced an impossible thermodynamic baseline onto the room, expressing what he would later codify as the *Kalorian Nova*.

**The Time Authorization.** Under Tier I Redactory law, Redactors cannot alter Time. Leonard did not melt time. Instead, the sheer, impossible coherence pressure of his thermodynamic Depth created a localized grammatical paradox. To prevent the *Kalorian Nova* from causing a catastrophic structural shear within the Stase-bubble, the Time Fragment made a strategic intervention — authorizing a local, micro-scale resumption of traversal. It allowed the heat to run its sequential course, forcing the explosion to exhaust itself instantaneously and shattering the local Stase-bubble.

Leonard was thrown clear of the wreckage. His Redactory was permanently stabilized. His mind was inextricably linked, from that moment forward, to the Stase and to Kerz.

## The Blasphemy and Flight

In Ardeatus, where civilizational doctrine is tightly bound to the Covenant and the mechanical orthodoxy of the Forge-Keepers, an unregistered Redactor capable of anomalous, Stase-breaking thermal generation is considered a theological and structural blasphemy. Realizing he would be hunted by the Ashborne Hunters or dissected by the Forge, Leonard took Kerz and fled across the border back into the Empire of Synus.

He presented himself for intake at the Apocachynthion in 2163.

## Abilities

### The Kalorian Nova

Leonard's signature technique is not a fire spell. It is the temporary deletion of the concept of thermal equilibrium in a localized space. He rewrites the local syntax of a room to mandate catastrophic molecular vibration — imposing an impossible thermodynamic baseline that the room then struggles to resolve. The air does not merely burn. It forgets that it was ever permitted to be cold.

He performs this without raising his voice. Without breaking his sentence.

### Resonance Sensitivity

Because he survived deep-Stase suspension, Leonard possesses profound resonance-sensitivity. He can passively read the structural and emotional coherence of those around him. This makes him a quiet, introverted, but exceptionally empathetic teacher — he frequently senses his students' Depth-strain or emotional distress long before it physically manifests.

### Kerz — the Companion

The mechanical crow Kerz remains permanently at his side. Due to their shared origin in the Stase, Leonard and Kerz share a closed-loop resonance link. Because Leonard is naturally quiet and often finds vocalization taxing after prolonged Dives, Kerz acts as his voice — communicating his intent through structural resonance that others perceive as spoken word.

## Present Status — Year 2200

Master von-Engelmeyer is one of the most respected and heavily attended instructors within the Apocachynthion's Kalt'sen Academy. He asks very little from the Empire, entirely eschews his House's politics, and dedicates his life to ensuring that young Redactors do not burn themselves alive in the dark.

The Apocachynthion has refused all Ardeatan extradition requests regarding him. It will continue to do so.

He is a man who survived the absence of Time by offering the universe an alternative it could not ignore.
