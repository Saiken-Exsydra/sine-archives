## What They Were

The **Skyphons**, called the **First Bearers** by the Church, were twelve persons formed through four joined conditions:

1. a bounded fragment externalized by Terra's Page Primordial;
2. a Precursor-engineered constraint body;
3. one Obscurial law-core co-addressed through a lunar seat;
4. Terra's acceptance through the All-Mother.

They were not manufactured as finished servants. Personhood emerged through sustained traversal, relation, disagreement, and contact with the laws they carried. A Bearer could refuse a Precursor or another Bearer when obedience would violate the Directive. The Page Primordial retained revocation of its authority; the Precursors did not own the persons it helped make.

The lunar law-core did not leave the Moon when a Skyphon walked Terra. Seat and Bearer formed one co-addressed Instrument. This let the twelve govern a changing world while the same jurisdictions remained load-bearing in the Intact Seal.

## The Fracture

The twelve did not dissolve in one identical event. They quarantined, partitioned, held, released, converted, vented, revealed, renewed, closed, preserved selfhood, made consequence legible, and protected lawful becoming. A Time Fragment kept those acts in the Twelve-Beat Hold while Syr'lene transferred her Anchor.

Each complete Bearer ended, but body, person, Page fragment, and Instrument entered different afterstates. Some law-cores remain seat-fast; some are distributed through the Ring; two were expelled to Terra; others are phase-lost. A surviving selection effect is not proof that its Skyphon remains a conscious person.

## What Institutions Know

The Church preserves names and witness. SiNE measures twelvefold phase structure without the history. The Apocachynthion holds artifact and failed-bearer evidence. The Empire has events and response law. Aurora, Ardeatus, and Elemental traditions preserve place-bound consequences. No institution holds the complete synthesis.

Reconstructing a Bearer is forbidden because no artifact, Dive, lineage, or office can recreate the authority, body, law-core, planetary acceptance, and lived personhood that formed one.
