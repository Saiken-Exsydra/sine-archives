## The Extraction Capital

Lu'mia is the administrative center of the **BrightCrystal extraction industry**, positioned just north of the Upper Scorch Belt. It is not primarily a military city — it is an industrial management city, housing the corporate and state apparatus that coordinates extraction operations across all three mining belts.

The **Extraction Commission of the Empire** operates from Lu'mia, as do the regional offices of SiNE's mining division and the technical bureaus that manage densification cycle monitoring. The decisions made in Lu'mia — which belts to work, which zones to rest, how to distribute raw crystal between civilian, military, and Redactory applications — shape the material foundation of everything the Empire builds, powers, and fields.

Population: approximately **700,000**.

---

## The Processing Complex

The city's eastern district contains the largest **BrightCrystal processing laboratory** in the Empire — a complex of sealed facilities where raw crystal undergoes refinement into the various grades used across civilian, military, and Redactory applications. The processing infrastructure is significant not only in scale but in the precision it requires: BrightCrystal refinement is a discipline where small errors in handling produce large consequences in output quality, and in extreme cases, structural consequences that the Apocachynthion monitors carefully.

## The Three Belts

Lu'mia administers extraction across three broad mining belts, each corresponding to a geological sub-zone defined by the intersection of mineral composition, radiation flux, and curvature-shear intensity:

The **Upper Scorch Belt** — the most accessible and most heavily industrialized, supplying the majority of the Empire's civilian energy infrastructure. Cities here are purpose-built industrial settlements, heavily shielded against radiation.

The **Mid Scorch Belt** — further toward the Bright Side, requiring specialized protective equipment. Higher-grade material for military applications and high-fidelity Redactory tools. Seismic instability and densification cycles are the primary operational hazards.

The **Deep Scorch Belt** — the frontier of extraction, where permanent facilities are impossible to maintain. Episodic high-intensity operations only: rapid deployment, maximum extraction, withdrawal. The highest-grade BrightCrystal produced anywhere in the Empire comes from here, and most of it is classified as a strategic military resource.

## The Landscape

The surrounding terrain is the classic pale, mineral-stony plateau of the southern extraction zone. The vegetation has long since given out. The sky is brighter here than in the Central Region — the accretion-disk glow more direct, the air drier, the light harder. The horizon shimmers during High Radiance season in a way that visitors from the north find unsettling and that Lu'mia's residents have long since stopped noticing.
