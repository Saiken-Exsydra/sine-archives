## What Obsidian Is

Obsidian is the Church's branch for identification, containment, and expulsion of demonic manifestation across the Twilight Band. It is also the branch that receives discovered Divine Vessels, processes recognized Diviners, supervises vessel training, governs Oracles, maintains Ecclesiastic Surgical standards, and preserves restricted Presence-facing doctrine.

Obsidian is not simply clergy with better training. It is four things at once:

- a vessel-governance system;
- an exorcistic field force;
- a restricted theological and medical apparatus;
- a pastoral formation built around Presence, Bearing, and identity erosion risk.

All recognized Church Diviners serve in Obsidian. No other Church branch independently trains, deploys, classifies, or supervises Diviners. Not everyone in Obsidian is a Diviner: non-vessel priests, confessors, Ecclesiastic Surgeons, Menders, Keepers, investigators, administrators, field commanders, and support personnel all serve inside the branch.

## Church Doctrine Carried by Obsidian

Obsidian does not hold the entire Church Codex inside its own record. It carries the parts of Church doctrine that become operational when a person, place, or presence must be assessed under pressure.

The transferred doctrine falls into five working areas:

- **Pathway doctrine**: demonic possession, angelic Presence, and the Gift of Sight all gather around the mortal threshold the Church calls the Pathway, but they are not the same phenomenon.
- **Possession taxonomy**: the Whispering, the Tenant, the Wearing, the Hollowed, and the Consumed form Obsidian's rescue-to-containment ladder.
- **Divine Vessel states**: Touched, Visited, Beatified, Crowned, Saint, Living Relic, and severe Crowned outcomes describe angelic Presence without borrowing demonic language.
- **Oracle doctrine**: the Gift of Sight is receptive, not projective. Oracles do not manufacture prophecy; they receive, testify, and are assessed through confession, consistency, and field reliability.
- **Rite and jurisdiction doctrine**: the Naming, the Rite of Expulsion, Grand Expulsion, Consecration of Place, and Desolation Event authority define when ordinary clergy end and Obsidian command begins.

This is why Obsidian records can read more practical than the Church Codex. They are not less theological. They are theology under field conditions.

## Possession, Presence, and Theophany

Obsidian's first task in a case is classification. Something overwhelming has touched a person; that does not yet say what kind of contact occurred.

A possession case moves through a different ladder than a Divine Vessel case. The **Whispering** and **Tenant** stages are still rescue-forward. **Wearing** requires full unit assessment because the vessel's own structure is being overwritten. **Hollowed** and **Consumed** cases are no longer treated as ordinary rescue; containment, closure, or northern-theatre response becomes the operative frame.

Angelic Presence uses different language. A person may be **Touched**, **Visited**, **Beatified**, or **Crowned** without being possessed. That distinction is absolute in Church doctrine and essential inside Obsidian. Presence can enlarge a vessel without consuming them, but enlargement is not safety. A Crowned vessel outside formation can still drift until domain begins organizing the person more than priesthood, memory, and relationship do.

The most dangerous classification word is **Theophany**. Obsidian does not use it casually. A suspected Theophany may be lawful divine expression, extreme Crowning, false Theophany, abyssal ingress, possession with unusual coherence, or a mixed case requiring Interior Council review. Until the classification is complete, Obsidian uses provisional language and keeps the case under hold.

## Rank Is Not Magnitude

**Rank** is institutional authority:

- **Postulant**: newly invested candidate in formation
- **Votary**: standard operational primary expulsor
- **Votary-Principal**: senior Votary trusted with leadership and formation duties
- **Prelate**: regional or formation command office below Rector-Ascendant
- **Rector-Ascendant**: highest regular field office

**Magnitude** is the scale of angelic Presence or source contact:

- **Gleam / Spark / Mote** contact: minor, brief, visionary, or early Presence.
- **Stellate** contact: lesser angelic office or fragment.
- **Cardinal** contact: major star within a Seraphic Constellation.
- **Cynosure** contact: the central Seraphic star itself.

That distinction matters. A Lucent is not automatically a commander. A Prelate is not automatically Lucent. A Rector-Ascendant is an office. **Answered** is a restricted theological category, not a routine promotion.

## The Seraphic Ladder

Obsidian explains its restricted angelic taxonomy through **Seraphic Constellations**.

```text
Cynosure
`-- the Seraphim itself
    `-- mortal vessel designation: Answered

Cardinal Star
`-- major governing star beneath the Seraphim
    `-- mortal vessel designation: Lucent

Stellate
`-- lesser angelic office or fragment
    `-- common operational designation: Stellate Diviner

Mote / Spark / Gleam
`-- minor contact, Beatification, or visionary phenomenon
```

The **Seven Answers** are the seven Seraphim with stable Church contact. The **Answered** are mortal vessels through whom one of those Seven Answers has answered.

## What Obsidian Power Looks Like

Obsidian does not publish a full public catalogue of domains, but its restricted records identify major operational patterns:

- **Light-domain** fusions strengthen radiance, disruption, and the Naming
- **Protection-domain** fusions reinforce barriers, shielding, and threshold defense
- **Healing-domain** fusions support vessel recovery and Ecclesiastic Surgery work
- **Authority-domain** fusions are rare and most visible among high command, Lucents, the Answered, and some Rector-Ascendants

This is why Diviners are not interchangeable. They are not stronger and weaker copies of one template. They carry different divine offices at different Magnitudes and in different states.

## Standard Formation

Every formal Obsidian operation is built around a three-person unit:

- **primary expulsor**: usually a Votary or above
- **confessor priest**: preparation, steadiness, and aftercare
- **Ecclesiastic Surgeon**: survivability assessment, fusion monitoring, and rescue judgment

Obsidian keeps these functions separate on purpose. It does not like force, care, and final judgment collapsing into one unchecked office.

The **Oracles** are Obsidian's sight-bearing intelligence body. In Obsidian operations they provide advance assessment, contact verification, and perceptual anchoring during high-tier engagements and Grand Expulsions.

## Obsidians and the Naming

Obsidian performs rites. Obsidian itself is not a rite.

Its signature operational technique is **the Naming**.

It is not only liturgical language. It is the act of addressing a hostile presence by its proper domain within the Sovereign's ordering of creation, using the disciplined Presence and Bearing of a Votary, Lucent, or Rector-Ascendant to make that address carry force.

That is why an Obsidian expulsor can do things a merely trained priest cannot.

## The Cost: Bearing Under Weight

Obsidian's honesty begins here.

Diviners change. Over years and decades, Presence reweights the vessel. This can be enlargement. It can also become drift.

Obsidian watches for the moment when:

- priesthood stops anchoring the domain
- the domain starts organizing the person

Its answer is continuous formation, confession, long-term pastoral accompaniment, and Ecclesiastic Surgeon monitoring. The goal is not to stop Presence. The goal is to keep the vessel themselves while Weight increases.

Danger scales with Magnitude and state. Stellate contact can erode a life. Cardinal contact can reorganize one. Cynosure contact can make the distinction between person and office difficult even for trained observers. Beatified vessels require discipline. Crowned vessels require supervision.

## Lucents and the Answered

### Lucent {#lucent}

A **Lucent** is a Diviner whose Magnitude reaches a **Cardinal Star** rather than a Stellate. Lucency is not honorary language. It is a restricted recognition that the vessel carries broader and more dangerous major-star Weight than ordinary Votaries can sustain.

### Answered {#answered}

The **Answered** are mortal vessels whose Magnitude reaches the **Cynosure**: the Seraphim itself as the central star of a Seraphic Constellation.

They are not the Seven Answers.
They are vessels through whom one of the Seven Answers has answered.

Halk'en Orveth belongs to this category.

## The Victorian Formation

The northern formation of Obsidian is the **Victorian Formation**.

It exists because the Darkmargin is not a sequence of isolated possession cases. It is a continuous theatre of pressure, entity emergence, treaty cooperation, and accelerated erosion risk.

The Victorian Formation works in sustained operational partnership with:

- the Cold Fleet
- HourGlass frontier commands
- northern Church formations
- informal StaSis counterparts in the SOE problem-space

Its central pastoral problem is plain: identity erosion accelerates in northern service.

## Institutional Meaning

Obsidian makes the Church's hierarchy easier to parse:

- **office** is one axis
- **branch membership** is another
- **rank** is another
- **Magnitude** is another
- **state** is another

A person can therefore be:

- a priest
- a Votary
- Beatified
- Stellate-aligned

Or:

- unordained
- a Rector-Ascendant
- Crowned
- Answered
- Cynosure-aligned under one of the Seven Answers

Those labels overlap. They do not replace one another.

## Why Obsidian Matters

Obsidian is the Church's answer to a problem ordinary clergy cannot solve:

How do you build an institution out of holy vesselhood without pretending vesselhood is safe?

Its answer is Investiture, formation, rank, Magnitude ledgers, confession, surgical monitoring, Oracle oversight, and a refusal to forget that Presence and erosion arrive together.
